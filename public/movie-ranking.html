My top animes of all time

The top three animes are:

Attack on Titan

If you love high action, blood, gore,plot twists,openings, closings, songs and animes with bad endings then this one is for you. I personally love this anime a lot due to it's interesting plot and peak fiction. Its easy to build an emotional connection with the characters but most of them end up dying anyways.

Naruto

This anime has a special place in my heart. The plot, the action, the characters and the music are all so cool. If you love ninjas then come watch Naruto where you will witness the life story of a young ninja who wants to become the head of the village.

Death Note

Murder, mystery, crime, death and a book + Anime with no bad episodes. This anime can become the most favourite of people who love detective genre.
